What you get
Everything a scanner
should be.
Instant scanning

Reads QR codes, EAN, UPC, Code 128, Code 39, Aztec, Data Matrix, PDF-417 and more. One camera frame is all it takes.

Code generator

Create QR codes and barcodes for URLs, contacts, Wi-Fi, email, SMS, geo locations, calendar events, and 1D barcode formats.

Fully offline

No internet required for scanning or generating codes. Everything is processed on-device. Your scans never touch a server.

Smart actions

Opens URLs, dials numbers, drafts emails, joins Wi-Fi, saves contacts, and opens maps — all from a single tap on the result.

Scan history

Every scan is saved locally with date, format, and content type. Search, filter, favorite, and add notes. Grouped by day for easy browsing.

My QR

Build your personal contact QR code from your name, phone, email, and address. Share it as a vCard. All contact data stays on your device.

Zero compromise privacy
Your scans.
Your device.

Easy QR Scanner stores all history, favorites, and settings locally in a SQLite database on your device. There are no servers, no analytics, no crash reporting, and no cloud sync. Uninstalling removes everything permanently.
